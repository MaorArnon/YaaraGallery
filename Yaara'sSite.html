About The Artist

Yaara Buda is an Israeli artist whose work sits at the unique intersection of mathematics, brain science, and visual expression. With a B.Sc. in Mathematics from Bar-Ilan University, her academic background informs her distinctive artistic perspective.

Currently working in the AI Research Division at Elbit Systems, Yaara brings her expertise in cognitive psychology and artificial intelligence into her creative process. Her art explores the fascinating relationship between computational systems and human perception.

Beyond her professional life, Yaara maintains a deep commitment to community service, volunteering with organizations that support women recovering from illness and at-risk youth. She is also an accomplished musician, playing harp and singing, which further informs her multimedia artistic approach.
